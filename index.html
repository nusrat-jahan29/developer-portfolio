Elsa's Powers

Manipulate Ice

Ability To. Psychically manipulate cold and ice.

Elsa can create, shape, and manipulate ice, water frozen into a solid state. As an elemental power, cryokinesis has a nigh-limitless number of uses, only limited by the imagination and natural limits.

Manipulate Snow

Granulation into snow. Ice Healing using snow.

Elsa can create,shape and manipulate snow, precipitation in the form of flakes of crystalline water ice. Because snow is composed of small ice particles, it is a granular material with an open and therefore soft structure, unless subjected to external pressure.
